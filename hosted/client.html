This is a possible prototype of the image-based chat system for the IGME 590 project. Laid out most of the base for image 'dialogue-boxes' used by player avatars and tested stamps. For now, it just randonly assigns and saves a random userID (1-1000) and assigns a randomized ink/overlay color for variety. In final, there will be player avatars moving in near-realtime and interacting with each other through images/icons (appearing overhead in 'speech-bubble').
